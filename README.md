# Elarion Bots

Autonomous agents designed to observe, predict and act across complex digital environments.

Part of the **Elarion Labs** ecosystem.

---

## Philosophy

Elarion Bots are built as autonomous systems capable of:

- observing market environments
- detecting signals and opportunities
- predicting potential outcomes
- executing actions automatically

The goal is to create agents that operate with minimal human intervention across complex systems.

---

## Current Focus

Initial research and development focuses on:

- prediction markets
- market making strategies
- signal detection
- automated execution

---

## Architecture

Typical bot architecture follows three core layers:

Observation  
Collects and processes data from external environments.

Prediction  
Analyzes signals and estimates possible outcomes.

Execution  
Acts on predictions by executing strategies.
